The [director
application](http://help.eclipse.org/latest/index.jsp?topic=/org.eclipse.platform.doc.isv/guide/p2_director.html)
is a headless way of performing some of the p2 operations such as
installing or uninstalling installable units. Given the appropriate
metadata, this application is capable of provisioning a complete
installation from scratch or simply extending your application.

Depending on the needs, this application can be executed inside or
outside of the application being provisioned. For complete documentation
on the director, including a full list of supported arguments and
related examples, see the official [p2 director
documentation](http://help.eclipse.org/latest/index.jsp?topic=/org.eclipse.platform.doc.isv/guide/p2_director.html).
